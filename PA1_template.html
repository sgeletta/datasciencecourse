###Section 1 Q2: Pre-Processing As could be seen from the above summary, the “date” field is a “factor”. This does need to change to a date field. In addition, a pre-processing step involving “tidying” up the data by creating two summary tables. The first summary table makes “date” the basic (atomic) record.
